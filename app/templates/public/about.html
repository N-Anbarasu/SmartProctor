About SmartProctor

SmartProctor is a professional browser-based online examination monitoring platform developed to help educational institutions conduct secure, transparent and reliable online assessments. By combining Artificial Intelligence, Browser Monitoring and Computer Vision, SmartProctor minimizes examination malpractice while providing an efficient experience for both teachers and students.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build an intelligent online examination platform that maintains academic integrity through automation, real-time monitoring, AI-assisted violation detection and detailed examination analytics.

Why SmartProctor?

Secure Examination

Protect examinations using browser monitoring and AI supervision.

AI Monitoring

Detect multiple faces, missing candidates and suspicious behaviour.

Live Supervision

Teachers can monitor every candidate from one dashboard.

Automatic Reports

Generate detailed reports after every examination session.

Analytics

View examination statistics and violation summaries.

Cross Platform

Runs on Windows, macOS, Linux and modern web browsers.

How SmartProctor Works

01. Teacher creates an online examination.
02. Students join using a secure room code.
03. Browser permissions are verified.
04. AI monitoring begins automatically.
05. Violations are detected and recorded.
06. Teachers receive real-time alerts.
07. Examination reports are generated instantly.
